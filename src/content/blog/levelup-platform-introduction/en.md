## What is Levelup?

Levelup is a quest-driven operating system for shipping ambitious work. We designed it for founders, ops leaders, and ecosystem builders who need tighter alignment without slowing their teams down. Instead of endless backlogs, Levelup curates focused questlines that describe the mission, expected impact, assets, and reward mechanics.

Each quest can include demo requirements, scorecards, essential Docs links, and built-in bounty pools. Contributors accept a quest, collaborate in multiplayer workstreams, and stream updates directly to stakeholders.

## Key features

- **Quest composer.** Bundle context, checkpoints, and guardrails into a single brief so contributors can start immediately.
- **Signal-rich updates.** Automations post recaps, XP earned, and blockers into the channels where leaders already live.
- **Reward OS.** Track XP, reputation, and payouts in one ledger that everyone can audit.

## Who it is for

Levelup powers product squads, accelerator partners, indie studios, and any community that lives on missions. If your people crave clarity plus the thrill of real-time rewards, Levelup keeps them in the loop and in the game.
